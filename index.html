About Me

I am an aspiring freelance web developer.

Salutations! My name is Charles Houston, but my friends call me Charlie. I'm a 26 year old husband and dog dad with a strong passion for web development. I really enjoy creating things, not just web applications. Once upon a time, I was a welder/pipefitter, a carpenter, and an independent contractor with a specialization in remodeling. I've been building things with my bare hands my entire life, and now I get the chance to build them with my mind!

In my free time, I like to travel and explore with my wife and two dogs. I like to play guitar, mountain bike, and read for fun. My family is very important to me, and I try to spend as quality time with them as possible. I believe that the most valuable thing in this world is time, and I try to spend mine wisely.
